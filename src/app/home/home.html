Welcome to FHIR Cloud

This is an open-source client for Fast Interoperability Healthcare Resources (FHIR) ® services. It is based on the current DSTU2 ballot draft, and while the client can target any DSTU2-compliant FHIR server, the particular focus is for testing interoperability of servers implementing the FHIR DAF Profiles.

Choose a FHIR Server

To get started, choose a FHIR server. There are plenty to choose from, and given the rapid pace of evolution of both the FHIR specification and its related implementations, there's a chance certain functionality may not be available in a given implementation.

Login

You will need to log in to access this application. If a server implements SMART on FHIR security, you will authenticate against their associated OpenID identity provider. Otherwise, you'll authenticate using your Google or LinkedIn account.

Explore

Use the left menu to explore the specification and try out different work flows. The visibility icon provides an under-the-covers look at the the JSON data returned in a particular interaction. And, of course, you can always use the developer tools of your browser-of-choice for an even deeper look at the interactions between this client and a particular FHIR server.
